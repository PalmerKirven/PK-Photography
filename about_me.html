    • About Me

    Palmer Kirven is a Lexington/Columbia based photographer. Born and raised in Lexington, South Carolina; attending school at University of South Carolina for a degree in Integrated Information Technology. Right now photography is a growing hobby for Palmer as he wishes to capture the world and share it’s beauty with whoever wants to view it. In the future, Palmer plans to pursue jobs and professional work with weddings, venues, and whatever else worth taking picture of. My Gear: Sony A7II, Sony 24-70mm, Sony 35mm, Cannon 70-300mm, GoPro Hero 11 mini, GoPro 12, 6” self-made FPV drone
